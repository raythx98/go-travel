## Surprising Discovery

`http.Flusher` does not propagate through middleware wrappers automatically. The `responseWriter` struct in `middleware/logging.go` needed an explicit `Flush()` method that delegates to the underlying writer — otherwise `w.(http.Flusher)` fails in the SSE handler and returns 500.
